How to use

  1. Open this page in a browser that has a Web3 wallet extension available.
  2. Connect your wallet and switch to Arc Testnet.
  3. Paste your compiled contract bytecode and ABI.
  4. Add any constructor arguments as a JSON array.
  5. Click Deploy Contract and wait for confirmation.

Use a contract compiler or existing Arc-contract bundle to generate the bytecode and ABI before deployment.
